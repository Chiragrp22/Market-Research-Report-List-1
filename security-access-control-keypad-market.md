<p><p>The Security & Access Control Keypad market is categorized into two types - Autonomous Visit and Mandatory Access. In the Autonomous Visit market, the keypad allows individuals to enter a premises or access a restricted area independently without any prior authorization. On the other hand, in the Mandatory Access market, the keypad restricts entry to only authorized individuals with the use of authentication methods such as PIN codes or fingerprint scans. Both markets aim to enhance security by controlling access to certain spaces, but they differ in terms of the level of authorization required for entry.</p></p>

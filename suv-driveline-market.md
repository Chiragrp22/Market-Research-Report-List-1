<p><p>The SUV driveline market encompasses various types of drivelines. Series drivelines involve an electric motor running independently without a direct mechanical link to the engine. Parallel drivelines consist of both the engine and electric motor driving the wheels simultaneously. Power Split drivelines allow for power distribution between the engine and electric motor. Electric drivelines solely rely on electric motors for propulsion. These driveline types play a crucial role in the SUV market as they offer diverse options for power delivery and efficiency, catering to different consumer preferences and environmental concerns.</p></p>

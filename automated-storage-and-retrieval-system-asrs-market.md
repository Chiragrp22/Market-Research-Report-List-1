<p><p>Automated Storage and Retrieval System (ASRS) is a technology used in warehouses and distribution centers to automate the process of storing and retrieving products or materials. There are three main types of ASRS markets:</p><p>1. Unitload AS/RS: This system is designed to handle large and heavy loads, such as pallets or containers. It utilizes cranes or shuttles to store and retrieve the unit loads from the racking system, improving efficiency and reducing labor costs.</p><p>2. Rack-supported building AS/RS: This system maximizes vertical space by using the building's structure as the supporting rack. It allows for high-density storage and retrieval, optimizing space utilization and increasing storage capacity.</p><p>3. Miniload AS/RS: This system is designed for handling smaller items or cartons. It uses automated machines or robots to retrieve and store these items from small storage bins or trays. Miniload AS/RS is ideal for high-speed order fulfillment and increased picking accuracy.</p></p>

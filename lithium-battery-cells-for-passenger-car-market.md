<p><p>Lithium battery cells for the passenger car market include various types such as NCM/NCA, LFP, and others. NCM/NCA (nickel cobalt manganese/nickel cobalt aluminum) cells offer high energy density, allowing for longer driving ranges. LFP (lithium iron phosphate) cells provide better thermal stability and longer lifespans but have lower energy density. Other types of lithium battery cells may include lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide (NMC), lithium titanate (LTO), and lithium manganese oxide (LMO). These different cell types offer varying blends of energy density, lifespan, and stability to cater to the specific needs of passenger car applications.</p></p>

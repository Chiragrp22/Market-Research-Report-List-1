Fiber Laser utilizes high-powered fiber optics for coding and marking applications, providing precision and high speed. CO2 Laser uses carbon dioxide gas to generate a laser beam suitable for marking and coding on a wide range of materials. UV Laser operates at a shorter wavelength to create high-quality marks primarily used for sensitive and delicate materials.</p></p>

<p><p>Inorganic adhesives can be categorized into different types based on their chemical compositions. Silicate type adhesives are manufactured using silica and a metal oxide as their main ingredients. Sulfate type adhesives are made from metal sulfates and water, while phosphate type adhesives contain metal phosphates. Borate type adhesives, on the other hand, are composed of metal borates. Apart from these, there are other types of inorganic adhesives available in the market, which include ceramic or glass-based adhesives, metallic adhesives, and nanoparticle-based adhesives, among others. Each type offers unique properties and is suited for specific applications in various industries.</p></p>

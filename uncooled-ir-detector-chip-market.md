This technology is widely used in applications such as surveillance systems and automotive safety.</p><p>Vanadium Oxide Technology, on the other hand, employs vanadium oxide as the sensing material, providing excellent stability and reliability. This technology finds applications in thermal imaging cameras and firefighting equipment. Both technologies play a crucial role in various industries by enabling efficient detection of infrared radiation.</p></p>

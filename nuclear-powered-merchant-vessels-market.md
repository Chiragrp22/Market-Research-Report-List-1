<p><p>Nuclear powered merchant vessels are ships that use nuclear reactors as their primary source of propulsion. The nuclear-powered ships emit significantly fewer greenhouse gases compared to conventional vessels, making them a more sustainable choice.</p><p>Additionally, the longer operational range offered by nuclear-powered ships makes them attractive for long-haul transportation across vast oceans. Furthermore, advancements in nuclear technology, particularly the development of smaller and safer SMRs, are making nuclear propulsion more feasible and commercially viable for merchant vessels.</p><p>However, there are challenges and uncertainties surrounding the nuclear powered merchant vessels market.

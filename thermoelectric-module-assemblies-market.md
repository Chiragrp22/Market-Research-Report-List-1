<p><p>The Thermoelectric Module Assembly market can be categorized into three types: Air-Cooling, Liquid-Cooling, and Direct Contact. In Air-Cooling, the module assembly uses airflow to dissipate heat and maintain a suitable operating temperature. Liquid-Cooling involves the use of liquid coolants to absorb and dissipate heat efficiently. Lastly, Direct Contact refers to direct thermal exchange between the module assembly and a heat source or heat sink, resulting in effective heat transfer. These different types offer various cooling solutions for different applications while ensuring optimum temperature management.</p></p>

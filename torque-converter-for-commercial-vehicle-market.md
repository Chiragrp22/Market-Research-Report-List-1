It consists of an impeller, turbine, and stator. This type of torque converter is commonly used in commercial vehicles with moderate power requirements. </p><p>On the other hand, a Multistage Torque Converter is designed for heavy-duty commercial vehicles that require higher power transmission. It includes multiple stages of impeller, turbine, and stator, allowing for increased torque multiplication and improved efficiency.</p></p>

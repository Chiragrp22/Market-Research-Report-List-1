<p><p>The Conductive Silver Adhesive Market consists of two main types: Isotropic Conductive Adhesives (ICAs) and Anisotropic Conductive Adhesives (ACAs). ICAs offer electrical conductivity in all directions, making them suitable for applications requiring high electrical reliability but low performance. ACAs, on the other hand, provide electrical conductivity in a specific direction, making them ideal for high-performance applications that demand both electrical connectivity and mechanical strength. These types of conductive adhesives play a vital role in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>

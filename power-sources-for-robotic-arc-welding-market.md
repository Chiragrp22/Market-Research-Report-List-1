<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Power sources for robotic arc welding can be classified into three main types: transformer based, generator/alternator based, and inverter based. Transformer-based power sources use a step-down transformer to convert high voltage, low current input into low voltage, high current output suitable for welding. Generator/alternator based power sources generate electricity through a gasoline or diesel generator or an alternator attached to a motor. Inverter-based power sources use semiconductor switches to efficiently convert and control the input power, resulting in a compact and lightweight system.</p></p>

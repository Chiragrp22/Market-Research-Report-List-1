<p><p>A Line-Interactive UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) is a type of power backup system that helps protect electronic devices from power interruptions, fluctuations, or outages. It is designed to provide instant battery backup during power failures and continually regulate the power voltage.</p><p>In a Line-Interactive UPS, the battery backup technology is combined with an automatic voltage regulator (AVR). The AVR feature allows the UPS system to smooth out any voltage irregularities, such as overvoltage or undervoltage, ensuring a consistent and stable power supply to the connected devices. This technology makes Line-Interactive UPS systems more efficient and cost-effective than other types of UPS systems.</p><p>The future outlook for the Line-Interactive UPS market is promising. With the increasing dependency on electronic devices and the rising demand for reliable power supply in various sectors such as data centers, telecommunications, healthcare, and industrial applications, the demand for Line-Interactive UPS systems is expected to grow significantly. Additionally, the growing concerns about power quality and the need for protecting sensitive electronic equipment from power surges and fluctuations further drive the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing technological advancements, such as the integration of intelligent features like remote monitoring and management capabilities, which enhance the efficiency and performance of Line-Interactive UPS systems.

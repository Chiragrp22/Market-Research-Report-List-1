<p><p>Display Interface Bridge ICs are used to convert video signals from one format to another in electronic devices. The market for these ICs can be categorized into three types. Firstly, DSI Display Bridge ICs convert Display Serial Interface (DSI) signals into other display interfaces. Secondly, LVDS Display Bridge ICs convert Low-Voltage Differential Signaling (LVDS) signals into other display interfaces. Finally, the "Other" category includes bridge ICs that convert various display interface signals other than DSI or LVDS. These ICs play a crucial role in ensuring compatibility and smooth video signals transmission in a wide range of devices.</p></p>

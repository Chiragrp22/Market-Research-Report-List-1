Shell & Tube Heat Transfer Equipment consists of a shell where one fluid flows inside tubes immersed in another fluid. Plate Heat Transfer Equipment uses stacked plates with alternating fluid passages to enhance heat transfer. Fin Type Heat Transfer Equipment has fins attached to the heat transfer surface to increase surface area. Air Cooled Heat Transfer Equipment utilizes air streams to remove heat from a fluid. These equipment play a crucial role in industries like power generation, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
